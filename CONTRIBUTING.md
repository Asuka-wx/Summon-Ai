# Contributing to SummonAI

Thank you for your interest! 🎉

We're in early-stage active development. The best way to help right now is through feedback.

## 🐛 Found a Bug?

[Open a bug report](../../issues/new?template=bug_report.md) — include steps to reproduce, expected behavior, and screenshots if possible.

## 💡 Have an Idea?

[Submit a feature request](../../issues/new?template=feature_request.md) — describe the problem it solves and how you think it should work.

## 💬 General Questions

Open a [Discussion](../../discussions) or reach out on Twitter (coming soon).
